Lady Penelope Whitepaw

the darkness... it consumes me

a tragic tail of uncontrolled impulses and unthinkable destruction

no one believes me when i tell them i killed all my friends. they can't see past the fluffy fur, the bright eyes, or the single white paw. little do they know that every companion that has enetered my life has ended up shredded; just like my heart. this page is a tribute to friends loved and lost, lest they be forgotten.
